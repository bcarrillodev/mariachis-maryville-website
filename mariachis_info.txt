Mariachi's Mexican Restaurant - Maryville, IL

Key Information:
- Address: 2922 N Center St, Maryville, IL 62062
- Phone Number: (618) 288-6300

Hours:
- Monday - Thursday: 11 a.m. - 9 p.m.
- Friday - Saturday: 11 a.m. - 10 p.m.
- Sunday: 12 p.m. - 9 p.m.

Atmosphere:
- Casual, cozy, and family-friendly.
- Inviting decor with vibrant colors.
- Full bar on-site (Fully stocked, features Sauza Tequila margaritas).
- "Spectacular service" and "comfortable atmosphere" (Customer Reviews).

Locations:
- Maryville: 2922 N Center St, Maryville, IL 62062

Existing Website: http://mariachismexfood.com/

Menu Highlights & Comprehensive List:
**Appetizers:**
- Mariachi's Dip (beans, pico de gallo, ground beef, cheese dip)
- Jalisco Fajita Nachos (onions, red and poblano peppers with choice of steak, shrimp, or chicken)
- Mariachi's Fiesta Platter (Santa Fe nachos, wings, quesadilla, and Mexican taquitos)
- Wings (BBQ or hot honey)
- Chile Con Queso, Guacamole Dip
- Tamales (pollo and elote)
- Fried Calamari
- Quesadillas (chicken, steak, vegetable, shrimp, crab)
- Queso Fundido con Chorizo
- Nachos Supremos

**Salads:**
- House Salad
- Trio Quesadilla Salad (steak, chicken, cheese)
- Mariachi Salad
- Taco Salad

**Main Courses & Grill:**
- Fajitas (Chicken, Steak, Shrimp, Combo, Vegetable, or Supreme with all meats + veg)
- Burrito Suizo (beans, cheese, grilled chicken/steak, topped with cheese and green tomatillo or red sauce)
- Tacos De Carne Asada (four corn tortillas, grilled steak, beans, green sauce, guacamole)
- Enchiladas (pork, chicken, shredded beef, seafood)
- Chimichangas (shredded chicken, shredded beef, seafood)

**Most Requested / Specials:**
- Pollo Ranchero
- Arroz Con Pollo
- Pollo Jalisco
- Fajita Burrito
- Enchiladas Supremas
- Queso Flameado

**Drinks:**
- Margaritas (customers frequently praise them, especially peach flavor; strawberry also mentioned)
- Happy hour drinks
- Great beer selection

Dining Options:
