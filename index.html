Experience Mariachi's

Casual, cozy, and family-friendly. We pride ourselves on spectacular service and an inviting atmosphere featuring vibrant decor and a full bar. Come by and visit us for delicious Mexican cuisine and tasty margaritas. Enjoy a taste of Mexico right here in Maryville!

Menu Highlights

Fajita Burrito

Our burrito kicked up a notch! Served on a sizzling hot skillet on a bed of onions, peppers, and covered with cheese sauce & ranchero sauce.

Santa Fe Nachos

Individual style nachos topped with beans & melted cheese. Your choice of grilled chicken or steak. Served with guacamole, pico de gallo, lettuce & sour cream.

Quesadillas

Large flour tortillas stuffed with mixed cheese. Served with guacamole, pico de gallo, lettuce & sour cream. Choice of Cheese, Vegetables, Chicken, Steak, Shrimp & Vegetables, or BBQ Chicken.

Combination Plates

Pick any 2 or pick any 3! Choose from enchilada, burrito, tamale, chile relleno, taco, mini chimi, or tostada. Served with rice and beans.
